About the Project

This project focuses on addressing the challenges sports teams face due to the need for multiple specialized apps, particularly smaller teams and communities with limited resources. By developing a cross-sport app, we aim to consolidate various functionalities into a single, user-friendly platform, reducing the reliance on multiple apps. This approach not only makes the app more affordable for smaller teams but also increases its appeal across different sports, fostering greater competition and expanding market share in a space with limited competition. The project will explore ways to enhance app functionality, ensuring it meets the diverse needs of users while maintaining affordability and accessibility.
